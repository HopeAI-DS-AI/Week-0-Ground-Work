# Week 0 - Ground Work

Before you start learning AI, Data Science, or coding — you must first prepare your mind and professional identity.

This week is all about getting ready in the right way— because the stronger your foundation, the faster your growth.

This week is very important in your career.It shapes how you think, learn, and present yourself as a future AI professional.

What You’ll Learn

- How to build a positive learning mindset— stay focused, motivated, and overcome challenges
- How to follow a daily study routine that builds consistency and discipline
- How to create a strong LinkedIn profile– headline, bio, featured section, and first post
- How to set up your GitHub profile – add projects and publish your first repository
- Setting your personal goals for the course (Mission Me!)

 Key Outcomes

 - You’ll feel confident, clear, and mentally ready to begin your AI journey
 - Your LinkedIn profile will reflect professionalism and growth
 - Your GitHub account will showcase your learning and first project
 - You’ll have a clear direction for your goals and learning roadmap

This isn’t just an introduction — it’s your career foundation week.

Everything you’ll achieve in AI starts from what you build here.
